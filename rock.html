Rock

Rock or rock-n-roll has an interesting background. It was defined differently, so different names were given for this genre, causing the full history to be a bit confused. However, in the 1950s,Chuck Berry was pinned the first real star of rock-n-roll. Rock was originally more R&B/Soul driven and eventually changed in the 60s, becoming more psychedelic and folk whilst introduced. After the breakups and deaths of band members, the 1970s had produced more hard rock bands to where the genre had become more experimental and popular, bringing the birth of soft and hard rock. Soft rock is a subgenre that is more melodic and gentle than the abrasive and energetic heavy rock music. In the 1980s, electronic dance music was thrown in to make modern rock, then 4 new genres of rock appeared with even more groups as well.
