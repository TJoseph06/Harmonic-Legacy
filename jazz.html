Jazz

Believe it or not, the tradition of jazz started not long before 1820 in Congo Square, which brought others from different countries together to New Orleans. Around this time, slaves would use this place as a space to gather and play music. The musical traditions of West Africa with rhythm, "feel", and blues from here and Europe having implemented harmonic cords and a variety of instruments are what created jazz entirely. The influence of New Orleans music is what played a crucial role in jazz rising to fame in the 1920s and developing it with the ethnic diversity and exchange in culture.
